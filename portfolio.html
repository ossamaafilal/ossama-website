How Dreadlocksmorocco was born

After graduating, I had no money and no plan. I was completely broke. One day during meditation, a message came from the universe: "Why not start a dreadlocks business?"

That is how Dreadlocksmorocco began. Since then, I have been offering dreadlocks services to amazing people and growing a strong, supportive community. A documentary is coming soon to share some of the beautiful stories behind this journey.

I am also working on launching a natural cosmetic brand made with Moroccan argan oil, focused on dreadlocks and natural hair care.

Thank you for being part of this vision. Welcome to the Dreadlocksmorocco community.
