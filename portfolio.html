How Dreadlocksmorocco was born

After graduating, I had no money, no plan — I was completely broke. One day, during meditation, a message came from the universe: "Why not start a dreadlocks business?"

That’s how Dreadlocksmorocco began. Since then, I’ve been offering dreadlock services to amazing people and growing a supportive community. A documentary is on the way to share some of the powerful stories behind this journey.

I’m also working on launching a natural cosmetic brand based on Moroccan argan oil, created for dreadlocks and natural hair care.

Thank you for being part of this vision — and welcome to the Dreadlocksmorocco community.
