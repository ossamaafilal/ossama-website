How Dreadlocks Morocco Was Born

After graduating, I had no money, no plan — I was broke. One day, during meditation, while I had my dreadlocks, a message came from the universe: “Why not start a dreadlocks business?”

That one moment changed everything. I followed the idea, and step by step, I created Dreadlocks Morocco — a business to help people maintain, repair, and celebrate their dreadlocks with love and care.

In the beginning, I worked with people from all walks of life. Their stories, our shared laughs, the lessons — these powerful moments inspired me to create a documentary, coming soon here in this portfolio.

But I didn’t stop there. Now, I’m also building something new: a natural cosmetic brand based on Morocco’s famous argan oil, made especially for dreadlocks and natural hair.

This is more than a business. It’s a spiritual journey, a healing process, and a way to build a community. Thank you for being part of it.
